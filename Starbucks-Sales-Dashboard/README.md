☕ Starbucks Sales Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The Starbucks Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across different countries, cities, and product categories. The dashboard helps identify revenue trends, profitable products, and regional performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing regions and cities.
- Discover the best-selling product categories.
- Track sales trends over time.
- Provide actionable business insights through interactive visualizations.

---

📊 Dashboard Features

- Executive KPI Cards
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Profit Analysis
- Country & City Performance
- Product Category Analysis
- Interactive Slicers
- Geographic Map Visualization
- Dynamic Filtering

---

📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Sales
- Profit Margin

---

💡 Business Insights

- Identified high-performing regions contributing the highest revenue.
- Compared product category performance to determine top-selling products.
- Analyzed sales trends to understand seasonal demand.
- Highlighted areas with lower profitability for business improvement.
